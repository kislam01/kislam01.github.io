About Me

Hello, world! My name is Kazi Tanzim Islam and I am a junior majoring in Computer Science in the School of Engineering at Tufts University. Born and raised in the city of Dhaka in Bangladesh, I moved to the United States in the year 2014 when I started college and discovered my passion for coding.

My primary field of interest is Web Development, but I also enjoy exploring various other areas in Computer Science through online learning platforms such as Coursera, edX and Udemy. Currently, I am using online resources to teach myself Android Application Development.

Outside academics, I am a music lover and an avid follower of soccer and cricket. When I am not coding, I can be found dabbling in the acoustic guitar or cheering away for Manchester United FC or the Bangladesh National Cricket Team.
